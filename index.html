India's first integrated program to recognize top expert cloud talents

About Cloud Masters

TPL Cloud Masters presents IT professionals in India an opportunity to emerge as Expert Cloud Talents. The talent that powers innovation using cloud technologies. A unique concept, Cloud Masters, sponsored by Google Cloud, is a platform for IT practitioners, developers and cloud enthusiasts to get recognized in a competitive and convivial environment with like-minded talents.

The program is a GCP Strategy Contest where select IT professionals from top organisations across India will grapple with use-case scenarios, answer multiple choice questions and get the chance to display their acumen in GCP solutions.

Conceptualised as a ‘fun while learning and sharing knowledge’ platform, Cloud Masters sponsored by Google Cloud provides IT professionals the chance to win awards, get recognition and network with the brightest minds in the industry. This program is a lifetime opportunity for IT professionals to establish their credentials as formidable Cloud Masters.

TPL Recap

TPL is India’s only IT strategy and technology contest featuring enterprise IT teams.

The participants—IT decision makers and influencers—explore and discover realistic business use cases of the sponsor’s technology solutions, even as they proactively seek finer details of those solutions. For the participants, it is a challenging and entertaining way of learning the latest technologies, as well as hone skills like collective thinking, ideating, strategizing, prioritizing, planning and team work.

Who can participate?

The contest is open to all ITDMs / IT Managers / Infra Managers / Security Managers / Network Managers / Data Scientists / Project Managers / Analysts / Application Developers, etc., from organizations in India. If you have demonstrated skills in the Cloud domain or an interest to master Cloud Technologies, this program is for you. Participants will be required to exhibit their knowledge of GCP solutions in business situations during the course of the program.

To be eligible, a participant must be from an organization based in India and must be working in its IT department. Participants must be full-time employees of the mentioned organization, or equivalent. Participants can be from diverse functions like IT planning, IT strategy, operations, databases, infrastructure, storage & networking, application development, support, MIS, etc., and have a demonstrated knowledge in Cloud technologies.

This is an invite only program and there is NO participation fee although organizers reserve the right to restrict or otherwise limit the participation of individuals based on specific qualifying criteria.

Google Cloud Masters Process

The TPL Cloud Master contest will be conducted online over 10 months

All participants intending to participate in the TPL Cloud Masters 2021 competition must register here

Participants are recommended to consume Google cloud use cases & content to enhance their knowledge

Participants take a 10 min quiz and score points

Participants in the top positions on leaderboard win prizes at end of month

Participants get 3 attempts in a month to improve score

The contest resets every month begins with a new scoreboard

Participants who do not win prizes can retry in the next month

Participants scoring top scores on the leaderboard get a Cloud Master certificate.
